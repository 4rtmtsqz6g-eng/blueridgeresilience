Individual Therapy

Healing that goes deeper than coping strategies.

I use AEDP (Accelerated Experiential Dynamic Psychotherapy), an approach built on the belief that you have an innate capacity for healing. Together, we explore what might be getting in the way of that, whether it's old patterns, unprocessed emotion, or the ways you've learned to protect yourself.

In our sessions, we pay close attention to what's actually happening for you, in real time. That's where change takes root. Not just as something you understand, but something you can feel.

Many people find that AEDP reaches places that other approaches haven't.
What I can help with
  • ◆Anxiety and worry that won't quiet down
  • ◆Depression and feeling disconnected
  • ◆Trauma and the effects of past wounds
  • ◆Grief and loss
  • ◆Low self-worth or persistent shame
  • ◆Difficulty trusting yourself or others
